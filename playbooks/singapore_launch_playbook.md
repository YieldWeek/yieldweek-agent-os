# Singapore Launch Playbook

## Objective

Validate YieldWeek in Singapore by proving paid organiser demand, sponsor interest, and city edition density.

## Primary validation gates

- 10 paid Type A badge listings
- 4 founding sponsors
- credible organiser pipeline
- first city edition calendar
- repeatable badge process

## Priority audiences

- event organisers
- DeFi and RWA protocols
- staking and validator infrastructure companies
- stablecoin and tokenized treasury issuers
- asset managers
- family office and allocator communities
- media partners

## Operating sequence

1. Build Singapore market map.
2. Identify top Type A badge targets.
3. Score candidate events.
4. Build sponsor target list.
5. Draft and approve outbound campaigns.
6. Run controlled outreach.
7. Classify replies and book calls.
8. Secure paid badges and sponsor commitments.
9. Lock event calendar.
10. Produce post-launch validation memo.

## Go / no-go standard

Continue only if paid demand is visible. Do not spend heavily on owned flagship production before organiser and sponsor validation.
