# Post-Event Report Playbook

## Objective

Turn every YieldWeek event into sponsor reporting, internal learning, and proof of category demand.

## Required sections

1. Event overview
2. Attendance summary
3. Audience quality notes
4. Sponsor deliverables delivered
5. Speaker and content recap
6. Operational issues
7. Commercial outcomes
8. Follow-up opportunities
9. Lessons learned
10. Next edition recommendations

## Required outputs

- internal post-mortem
- sponsor-ready recap
- CRM updates
- follow-up task list
- reusable proof points

## Reality check rule

Do not inflate attendance, sponsor outcomes, or allocator quality. Separate measured facts from qualitative impressions.
