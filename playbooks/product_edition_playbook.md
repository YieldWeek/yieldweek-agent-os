# Product / Edition Playbook

## Objective

Define what a YieldWeek edition is and make the format repeatable across cities.

## Core gates

- Singapore edition format documented before launch
- v1 edition playbook approved before Abu Dhabi planning begins
- post-Singapore update completed within 30 days of close
- franchise-ready status only after founder approval

## Required sections

- edition format
- attendee experience
- sponsor experience
- agenda structure
- quality criteria
- operations standard
- data capture standard
- post-event review process
- franchise readiness status

## Decision standard

Replicability over novelty. If it cannot be documented and repeated, it is not yet a product standard.
