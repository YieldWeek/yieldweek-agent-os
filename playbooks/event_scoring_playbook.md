# Event Scoring Playbook

## Objective

Evaluate whether an event should be pursued, certified, ignored, or rejected.

## Score dimensions

Score each dimension from 1 to 5.

- Yield relevance
- Audience quality
- Organiser credibility
- Sponsor fit
- Speaker credibility
- Calendar fit
- Brand risk
- Commercial probability
- Operational complexity

## Recommendation logic

- 40+ = priority target
- 32-39 = strong target, needs review
- 24-31 = monitor or low-priority outreach
- below 24 = reject or ignore

## Mandatory reject flags

Reject or escalate if:

- unclear organiser identity
- obvious scam or low-quality brand association
- misleading financial claims
- retail-only hype with no professional relevance
- reputationally sensitive sponsor or speaker risk
- conflict with existing YieldWeek commitments
