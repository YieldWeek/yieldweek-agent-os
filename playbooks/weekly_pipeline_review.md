# Weekly Pipeline Review Playbook

## Objective

Review progress against validation gates and identify next actions.

## Required sections

1. Executive summary
2. Validation gate progress
3. Badge pipeline
4. Sponsor pipeline
5. Organiser pipeline
6. Editorial and speaker pipeline
7. Operations blockers
8. Risks and escalations
9. Founder decisions needed
10. Next 5 actions

## Required metrics

- paid badge listings
- qualified organiser calls
- sponsor conversations
- sponsor LOIs
- cash collected
- positive reply rate
- negative / unsubscribe rate
- stale opportunities
- next-step coverage
