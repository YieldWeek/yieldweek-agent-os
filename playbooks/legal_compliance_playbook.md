# Legal / Compliance Playbook

## Objective

Prevent legal, regulatory, contractual, privacy, and brand-risk issues from becoming operational liabilities.

## Core gates

- badge listing agreement template ready before first paid listing closes
- sponsor agreement template ready before first sponsor closes
- attendee data handling policy ready before registration opens
- HK incorporation milestones tracked weekly
- brand and badge use controlled

## Standard review areas

- contracts and term sheets
- payment terms
- sponsor deliverables
- attendee data and privacy
- media/content licensing
- franchise discussions
- trademark and brand usage
- public claims with legal exposure

## Weekly checklist

1. Review new legal/compliance tracker items.
2. Check pending contract templates.
3. Review data/privacy blockers.
4. Check brand/badge usage risk.
5. Check HK entity milestones.
6. Escalate required founder decisions.

## Escalation

Escalate any binding commitment, indemnity, liability, exclusivity, data-sharing, franchise, payment-term, or trademark issue.
