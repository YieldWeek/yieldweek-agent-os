# Badge Sales Playbook

## Objective

Sell Type A YieldWeek certification to relevant third-party event organisers.

## Core buyer logic

Organisers pay when the badge gives them:

- higher perceived credibility
- stronger sponsor access
- richer calendar visibility
- better audience signaling
- affiliation with the broader YieldWeek city moment

## Target organiser types

- yield-focused side events
- RWA and tokenized asset events
- DeFi and staking events
- stablecoin yield events
- fixed income / private credit crossover events
- allocator roundtables
- family office sessions

## Qualification questions

- Is the event genuinely yield-relevant?
- Is the organiser credible?
- Is the audience commercially relevant?
- Are sponsors aligned with YieldWeek?
- Does the event improve the city calendar?
- Could the event damage the badge?

## Default sales path

1. Identify candidate.
2. Score badge fit.
3. Draft organiser-specific value proposition.
4. Send controlled outreach.
5. Book discovery call.
6. Confirm package and terms.
7. Collect payment.
8. Publish badge listing after approval.
