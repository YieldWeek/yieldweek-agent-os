# Sponsor Sales Playbook

## Objective

Convert relevant companies into paid YieldWeek edition sponsors.

## Sponsor value proposition

YieldWeek helps sponsors reach a concentrated audience around cross-asset yield finance: organisers, protocols, asset managers, allocators, platforms, service providers, and media.

## Sponsor categories

- DeFi protocols
- RWA platforms
- staking infrastructure
- stablecoin issuers
- tokenized treasury issuers
- asset managers
- custodians
- wallets
- exchanges
- data and infrastructure providers
- legal, audit, compliance, and advisory firms

## Package logic

Do not sell generic logo placement first. Sell category ownership, qualified relationship access, edition-level visibility, and narrative relevance.

## Default sales path

1. Research account.
2. Define fit and sponsorship angle.
3. Draft outreach.
4. Reality-check claims.
5. Send approved campaign.
6. Book discovery call.
7. Prepare founder call brief.
8. Confirm package.
9. Collect payment.
10. Deliver and report.
