# Media / Distribution Playbook

## Objective

Build category credibility for YieldWeek through consistent, relevant, and approved distribution.

## Core gates

- LinkedIn cadence live before Singapore launch
- newsletter issue published before Edition 1
- media partner relationship log maintained
- press/milestone announcements approved before release

## Channel priorities

1. Olivier personal LinkedIn
2. YieldWeek company page
3. YieldWeek newsletter
4. TheBigWhale / media partners
5. Targeted sponsor and organiser follow-up

## Weekly checklist

1. Review approved editorial assets.
2. Build distribution plan.
3. Run reality/legal checks where needed.
4. Schedule approved content.
5. Track inbound and engagement signals.
6. Brief Commercial Agent on outreach windows.

## Decision standard

Relevance over reach. Institutional credibility beats generic follower growth.
