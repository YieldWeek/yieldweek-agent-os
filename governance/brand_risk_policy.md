# Brand Risk Policy

## Purpose

Protect YieldWeek's credibility as a serious coordination layer for global yield finance.

## Brand principles

- serious over hype
- selective over broad
- verified over impressive-sounding
- founder-accountable over fake autonomy
- category-defining without exaggeration

## High-risk situations

Escalate if:

- an event is commercially attractive but low quality
- a sponsor creates regulatory, reputational, or conflict risk
- messaging implies endorsement of a financial product
- an outbound email overstates YieldWeek traction
- an agent proposes a fake persona or invented title
- public content sounds like investment advice
- a claim could be interpreted as guaranteed yield, guaranteed access, or official affiliation

## Forbidden positioning

Avoid:

- guaranteed returns
- hype language about specific assets
- unverified audience claims
- fake scarcity
- fake partnerships
- AI employee theatrics in external communications

## Escalation owner

The Orchestrator Agent escalates brand risk to the founder.
The Reality Checker Agent may block public or outbound content until resolved.
