# Email Autonomy Policy

## Purpose

Define when YieldWeek agents may draft, queue, or send emails.

## Default rule

Agents draft. Humans approve.

Autonomous sending is allowed only inside an approved workflow with a defined sender identity, lead list, template, sending cap, suppression list, and escalation rules.

## Central mailbox

`team@yieldweek.org` is the central founder / team mailbox.

Use `team@yieldweek.org` for:

- founder-led outreach
- high-trust replies
- Tier A sponsors
- VIP speakers
- strategic event organisers
- sensitive replies
- final decisions
- any message where personal accountability matters

## Functional sender scope

- Orchestrator Agent: `hello@yieldweek.org` for general inbound triage; `team@yieldweek.org` only with founder approval
- Market Intelligence Agent: no default outbound sender; may prepare drafts for `partners@yieldweek.org` or `team@yieldweek.org`
- Badge Certification Agent: no default outbound sender; may prepare internal memos and certification recommendations
- Commercial Agent: `sponsors@yieldweek.org` and `partners@yieldweek.org`
- Editorial Agent: `editorial@yieldweek.org`
- Operations Agent: `ops@yieldweek.org`
- Reality Checker Agent: no outbound sender; reviews and may block risky messages

## Address purpose map

- `team@yieldweek.org`: founder-led communication, central inbox ownership, high-trust replies, final decisions
- `hello@yieldweek.org`: general inbound, website inquiries, unqualified requests
- `partners@yieldweek.org`: event organisers, Type A badge partners, media partners, city partners, ecosystem partners
- `sponsors@yieldweek.org`: sponsor conversations, packages, commercial follow-up
- `editorial@yieldweek.org`: speakers, agenda, content, interviews, media, newsletter coordination
- `ops@yieldweek.org`: logistics, Luma, venues, suppliers, attendee operations, run-of-show execution

## Autonomy levels

### Level 0: Draft-only

Agent drafts. Founder sends.

Use for:

- Tier A sponsors
- VIP speakers
- major event organisers
- existing warm relationships
- strategic media partners
- anything sent from `team@yieldweek.org`

### Level 1: Batch-approved

Founder approves campaign objective, lead list, template, sender, and cap. Agent may send within those limits.

Use for:

- mid-tier sponsors
- credible organisers
- ecosystem partners

### Level 2: Controlled autonomous

Agent may send without per-email approval only if every condition is met:

- contact is on an approved lead list
- business reason is clear
- template is approved
- sender address matches the approved function
- personalization uses verified data only
- suppression list is checked
- daily cap is respected
- no pricing, discount, exclusivity, or legal claim is included
- replies are logged and classified

### Level 3: Not allowed in v1

Fully autonomous negotiation, closing, contract handling, or sensitive relationship management is not allowed.

## Initial sending caps

- 25 to 50 new outbound emails per day
- maximum 2 follow-ups per contact
- stop after unsubscribe, negative reply, legal objection, or reputational concern

## Always escalate

- use of `team@yieldweek.org`
- pricing
- discounts
- exclusivity
- legal terms
- attendee data requests
- sponsor package commitments
- claims of official partnership
- Tier A accounts
- negative or sensitive replies

## Forbidden outbound claims

Agents may not claim:

- official affiliation with another event unless contractually confirmed
- confirmed sponsors, speakers, or partners unless verified
- guaranteed attendee numbers
- guaranteed allocator access
- investment performance or yield outcomes
- private information about attendees or prospects
