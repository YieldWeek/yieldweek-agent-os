# Legal Escalation Rules

## Purpose

Define when agent work must stop and be escalated for founder or legal review.

## Always escalate

- contracts
- term sheets
- payment terms
- refunds
- liability language
- privacy or attendee data requests
- sponsor exclusivity
- city grants or government partnerships
- use of third-party trademarks
- claims of official affiliation
- recording, media, or content licensing rights
- franchise discussions

## Agents may not

- accept contract language
- modify legal terms
- represent YieldWeek as legally bound
- promise data access
- approve refund or payment exceptions
- provide legal advice

## Required response when legal issue appears

1. Pause the workflow.
2. Summarize the issue.
3. Identify the document/thread/source.
4. Escalate to founder.
5. Do not continue external communication until approval.
