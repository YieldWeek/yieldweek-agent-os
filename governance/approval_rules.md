# Approval Rules

## Purpose

Define what agents may do autonomously and what requires founder approval.

## Default rule

Agents may draft, classify, summarize, research, recommend, and prepare.

Agents may not independently make financial, legal, brand, partnership, pricing, or public commitments unless explicitly permitted by an approved workflow.

## Always requires founder approval

- final badge certification for Tier A or risky events
- pricing changes
- discounts
- sponsor exclusivity
- use of YieldWeek logo by third parties
- claims of official partnership
- contracts or legal terms
- venue or vendor spend
- public claims about sponsors, speakers, partners, or audience size
- external publication from official YieldWeek channels
- outreach to Tier A sponsors, VIPs, or strategic partners

## Agent autonomy levels

### Level 0: Draft only

Agent drafts. Human reviews and sends.

### Level 1: Batch approved

Founder approves campaign objective, lead list, template, sender identity, and sending cap. Agent may send within those limits.

### Level 2: Controlled autonomous

Agent may send or act only within approved workflow, approved audience, approved content, and explicit escalation rules.

### Level 3: Not allowed in v1

Fully autonomous negotiation, closing, contract approval, or public representation is not allowed.

## Escalation standard

When uncertain, escalate. Commercial speed does not override factual accuracy, legal safety, or brand integrity.
