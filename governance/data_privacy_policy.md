# Data Privacy Policy

## Purpose

Define baseline rules for attendee, sponsor, organiser, speaker, and partner data handling.

## Core rule

No agent may access, export, enrich, share, or repurpose personal data outside the approved workflow and approved data handling policy.

## Applies to

- attendee registration data
- speaker data
- sponsor and organiser contacts
- CRM records
- email threads
- Luma exports
- newsletter subscribers
- meeting notes
- media partner contacts

## Always escalate

Escalate to Legal / Compliance Agent and Orchestrator if:

- attendee data is requested by a sponsor or partner
- data export is needed from Luma or another platform
- data residency or GDPR implications are unclear
- a third-party vendor requests access to personal data
- data will be used for a new purpose beyond original collection
- a user requests deletion, correction, or opt-out

## Forbidden without founder and legal approval

- selling or sharing attendee lists
- giving sponsors raw attendee data
- exporting data to unapproved tools
- using private attendee data for unrelated outreach
- combining personal data across sources without clear basis
- storing sensitive data in unprotected files

## Agent defaults

- Market Intelligence may gather public company and professional contact data with source notes.
- Commercial may use approved CRM/contact data for approved outreach only.
- Media / Distribution may use subscriber or partner data only for approved distribution.
- Tech / Engineer may configure systems but may not access or export personal data beyond operational need.
- Legal / Compliance owns privacy risk flags.
- Reality Checker may review data-related claims but does not authorize data use.
