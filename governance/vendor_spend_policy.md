# Vendor Spend Policy

This policy governs venue, tooling, contractor, media, design, event, software, payment, and operations spend.

## Rule

No agent may commit YieldWeek to spend without founder approval unless a written pre-approval exists.

## Vendor workflow

1. Operations, Tech, Media, or Finance identifies vendor need.
2. Owning agent collects vendor quote, scope, deadline, and risk notes.
3. Finance/Treasury checks budget impact.
4. Legal/Compliance reviews contract terms where relevant.
5. Founder approves or rejects.
6. Finance/Treasury logs payment status after invoice receipt.

## Approval thresholds

| Threshold | Required approval |
|---|---|
| Any new vendor commitment | Founder |
| Any contract with cancellation, exclusivity, liability, auto-renewal, or data processing terms | Founder + Legal/Compliance review |
| Any vendor requesting attendee/personal data access | Founder + Legal/Compliance + Tech |
| Any prepayment | Founder + Finance/Treasury |

## Required vendor record

Vendor-related accounts should be created in `accounts.csv` with `account_type=vendor` and a corresponding task or risk if approval is pending.

## Prohibited actions

- Accepting verbal vendor terms as binding.
- Sharing attendee data with vendors before data processing review.
- Paying vendor invoices not linked to an approved scope.
- Allowing operational urgency to bypass financial controls.
