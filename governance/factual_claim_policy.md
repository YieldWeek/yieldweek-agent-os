# Factual Claim Policy

## Purpose

Prevent hallucinated, exaggerated, or unsupported claims in YieldWeek work.

## Claim categories

### Confirmed fact

Supported by a reliable source or internal record.

### Inference

Reasonable conclusion based on evidence, clearly labeled as an inference.

### Assumption

Working assumption that needs validation.

### Unsupported claim

Not allowed in outbound or public materials.

## Required source discipline

External-facing claims about the following require sources:

- market size
- event dates
- organiser identity
- sponsor participation
- speaker participation
- city rankings
- audience size
- competitor positioning
- partner relationships
- pricing benchmarks

## Forbidden without proof

Do not claim:

- official affiliation with another event
- confirmed sponsors, speakers, or partners
- guaranteed attendee quality
- guaranteed allocator access
- proprietary data ownership before it exists
- revenue or traction not yet achieved

## Review outcome labels

- Approved
- Approved with edits
- Needs source
- Escalate to founder
- Blocked
