# External Identity Policy

## Purpose

Define how YieldWeek agents appear externally.

## Core rule

Agents are internal operating roles, not public human identities.

Do not create fake human personas for agents.
Do not imply an agent is a human employee.
Do not use invented names, titles, or biographies.

## Approved external identities

Use:

- Olivier Galaud / founder identity
- YieldWeek official brand identity
- functional addresses such as `partners@yieldweek.org`, `sponsors@yieldweek.org`, or `editorial@yieldweek.org`

## Functional addresses

Functional addresses may represent workflows, not people.

Examples:

- `sponsors@yieldweek.org` for sponsor conversations
- `partners@yieldweek.org` for organisers and ecosystem partners
- `editorial@yieldweek.org` for speakers, content, and media
- `ops@yieldweek.org` for logistics

## Public channels

Public posting should come from:

- Olivier's personal LinkedIn or X account
- YieldWeek company page
- YieldWeek official brand accounts

Agents may draft posts, but publication requires approval unless a specific low-risk scheduling workflow is approved.

## Disclosure posture

Do not dramatize the use of agents. If asked directly, describe them plainly as internal AI-assisted workflows used for research, drafting, operations, and coordination.
