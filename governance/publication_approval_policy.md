# Publication Approval Policy

This policy controls public-facing YieldWeek content.

## Scope

Applies to website copy, LinkedIn posts, newsletters, press releases, sponsor announcements, badge announcements, event pages, media partner statements, and any public claim about YieldWeek traction, sponsors, attendees, partners, revenue, or market position.

## Approval chain

1. Editorial drafts the content.
2. Reality Checker reviews factual claims and source support.
3. Legal/Compliance reviews regulated, contractual, privacy, exclusivity, or partnership-sensitive claims.
4. Media/Distribution schedules only after approval is explicit.
5. Founder approval is required for high-impact public claims.

## Claims requiring founder approval

- Sponsor or partner announcement.
- Revenue, attendee, pipeline, or traction claim.
- Badge certification announcement.
- City launch announcement.
- Legal, regulatory, or compliance claim.
- Any claim implying endorsement by a government, regulator, issuer, sponsor, or media partner.

## Required publication log

Every publication must log:

- content title
- channel
- publishing date
- approval owner
- Reality Checker status
- Legal/Compliance status where relevant
- source URLs for factual claims

## Correction protocol

If a false or unsupported claim is detected after publication:

1. Create a `risks.csv` entry with severity high or critical.
2. Notify Orchestrator and founder.
3. Remove, correct, or annotate the public claim.
4. Publish correction if external reliance is likely.
5. Add lesson to publication approval checklist.
