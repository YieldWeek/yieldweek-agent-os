# Singapore Smell Test Scorecard

Singapore is the first operating test for YieldWeek Agent OS. The scorecard exists to prevent narrative drift and force measurable validation.

## Scoring model

| Category | Weight | Green | Yellow | Red | Owner |
|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Badge revenue | 20% | 10 paid badge listings | 5-9 paid listings | <5 paid listings | Commercial + Badge Certification |
| Sponsor revenue | 25% | 4 founding sponsors | 2-3 sponsors | <2 sponsors | Commercial |
| Qualified pipeline | 15% | 30 qualified sponsor accounts | 15-29 accounts | <15 accounts | Market Intelligence |
| Organiser/event quality | 10% | 10 credible Type A event targets | 5-9 targets | <5 targets | Market Intelligence + Badge Certification |
| Legal readiness | 10% | Contract and GDPR workflows live | Minor template gaps | No legal process | Legal/Compliance |
| Finance readiness | 10% | Invoice and reconciliation live | Manual but documented process | No invoice process | Finance/Treasury |
| Product repeatability | 10% | Edition playbook baseline produced | Partial lessons captured | No repeatable standard | Product |

## Go/no-go interpretation

- Green overall: proceed to next city expansion.
- Yellow overall: continue Singapore but do not scale until root causes are fixed.
- Red overall: stop multi-city planning and re-underwrite the business model.

## Required weekly inputs

- `opportunities.csv`: pipeline by stage and value.
- `invoices.csv`: invoice/payment/reconciliation status.
- `events.csv`: badge targets and event quality status.
- `risks.csv`: open high/critical risks.
- `tasks.csv`: P1/P2 blockers.

## Founder decision gates

Founder approval is required before:

- first paid badge listing
- first paid sponsorship
- first public sponsor announcement
- first attendee registration page launch
- any franchisee activation
