# Contract Legal Review Workflow

## Purpose

Draft, review, and control legal and contractual materials before they are used externally.

## Trigger

A badge agreement, sponsor agreement, speaker agreement, vendor agreement, franchise agreement, or risky commercial term is needed.

## Agents involved

- Primary agent: Legal / Compliance Agent
- Supporting agents: Commercial Agent, Finance / Treasury Agent, Product Agent, Franchisee Manager Agent, Reality Checker Agent, Orchestrator Agent

## Inputs

- Agreement type
- Counterparty type
- Commercial terms
- Payment terms
- Deliverables
- Data/privacy implications
- Brand usage implications
- Jurisdiction or city

## Steps

1. Define legal need
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: contract request brief

2. Draft template or review term
   - Owner: Legal / Compliance Agent
   - Output: draft or risk notes

3. Align payment terms
   - Owner: Finance / Treasury Agent
   - Output: invoice/payment term check

4. Align commercial deliverables
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: sponsor/badge deliverable check

5. Align product/format constraints
   - Owner: Product Agent when relevant
   - Output: format/playbook consistency check

6. Reality and brand check
   - Owner: Reality Checker Agent
   - Output: unsupported claims or brand risk flags

7. Founder approval
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: approved, revised, or blocked document

## Human approval points

Founder approval required before any contract, term sheet, agreement template, franchise material, indemnity, liability, exclusivity, data sharing, or refund term is used externally.

## Outputs

- Contract request brief
- Draft template or term review
- Risk notes
- Founder approval status
- Final approved template version

## Success metrics

- templates ready before first deal close
- no unapproved legal commitments
- data/privacy issues flagged before registration opens
- finance terms aligned with invoicing workflow
