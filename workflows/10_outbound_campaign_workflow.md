# Outbound Campaign Workflow

## Purpose

Run controlled outbound campaigns at scale without damaging YieldWeek's brand, deliverability, or legal/commercial posture.

## Trigger

A founder-approved outbound campaign is created for sponsors, organisers, speakers, media partners, or city partners.

## Agents involved

- Primary agent: Commercial Agent
- Supporting agents: Market Intelligence Agent, Reality Checker Agent, Orchestrator Agent

## Inputs

- Campaign objective
- Approved lead list
- Sender identity
- Approved template
- Personalization fields
- Sending cap
- Follow-up cadence
- Suppression list
- Escalation rules

## Steps

1. Campaign brief
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: approved campaign objective and scope

2. Lead list validation
   - Owner: Market Intelligence Agent
   - Output: verified leads and contact paths

3. Template preparation
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: sequence draft and personalization logic

4. Risk review
   - Owner: Reality Checker Agent
   - Output: approved or edited campaign assets

5. Founder approval
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: campaign approval, sending cap, and restrictions

6. Send campaign
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: emails sent, CRM updated

7. Reply handling
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: classified replies and recommended next actions

8. Weekly performance review
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: campaign metrics and changes needed

## Human approval points

Founder approval required before first send, before any template change, before contacting Tier A accounts, and before any pricing or commercial commitment.

## Forbidden claims

Outbound may not claim:

- official affiliation with an event unless contractually confirmed
- confirmed sponsors, speakers, or partners unless verified
- guaranteed allocator access
- guaranteed attendee numbers
- exclusivity unless approved
- pricing discounts unless approved

## Sending caps

Initial recommended cap:

- 25 to 50 new outbound emails per day
- maximum 2 follow-ups per contact
- stop immediately after unsubscribe, negative reply, or legal objection

## Outputs

- Campaign brief
- Approved lead list
- Approved email sequence
- Campaign log
- Reply classifications
- Performance review

## Success metrics

- positive reply rate
- qualified calls booked
- unsubscribe / complaint rate
- conversion to paid listing or sponsor conversation
- no material brand incidents
