# Speaker Invitation Workflow

## Purpose

Identify, qualify, invite, and coordinate relevant speakers for YieldWeek city editions.

## Trigger

A city edition, panel, roundtable, or content theme requires speaker candidates.

## Agents involved

- Primary agent: Editorial Agent
- Supporting agents: Market Intelligence Agent, Commercial Agent, Reality Checker Agent, Operations Agent

## Inputs

- City edition
- Topic or panel theme
- Target speaker profile
- Existing relationships
- Invitation deadline

## Steps

1. Speaker mapping
   - Owner: Market Intelligence Agent
   - Output: candidate speaker list with source links

2. Editorial fit
   - Owner: Editorial Agent
   - Output: ranked speaker shortlist

3. Commercial conflict check
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: sponsor/conflict notes

4. Invitation draft
   - Owner: Editorial Agent
   - Output: speaker invitation email

5. Claim and commitment review
   - Owner: Reality Checker Agent
   - Output: approved or flagged invitation

6. Logistics handoff
   - Owner: Operations Agent
   - Output: accepted speaker coordination checklist

## Human approval points

Founder approval required for Tier A speakers, public agenda commitments, VIPs, controversial figures, or sponsor-linked speaker requests.

## Outputs

- Speaker shortlist
- Invitation draft
- Speaker status tracker update
- Logistics checklist

## Success metrics

- accepted speakers
- audience relevance
- sponsor relevance without editorial compromise
- low cancellation risk
