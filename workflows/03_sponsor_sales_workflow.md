# Sponsor Sales Workflow

## Purpose

Convert relevant companies into paid YieldWeek edition sponsors.

## Trigger

A company is identified as a sponsor prospect for a YieldWeek city edition.

## Agents involved

- Primary agent: Commercial Agent
- Supporting agents: Market Intelligence Agent, Editorial Agent, Reality Checker Agent, Orchestrator Agent

## Inputs

- Sponsor name
- Website
- Target city or edition
- Sponsorship category
- Known buyer/contact path
- Current package hypothesis

## Steps

1. Account research
   - Owner: Market Intelligence Agent
   - Output: Sponsor brief with source links.

2. Strategic fit
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: ICP fit score, buyer hypothesis, budget hypothesis.

3. Narrative alignment
   - Owner: Editorial Agent
   - Output: Suggested sponsorship angle and content relevance.

4. Outreach draft
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: Email sequence.

5. Risk review
   - Owner: Reality Checker Agent
   - Output: Verified outbound draft.

6. Approval or send decision
   - Owner: Orchestrator Agent
   - Output: Approve batch, escalate, or block.

7. Reply classification
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: Next action.

8. Founder call prep
   - Owner: Commercial Agent
   - Output: Call brief, objections, proposed ask.

## Human approval points

Founder approval required for:

- Tier A sponsors
- pricing
- exclusivity
- logo usage
- claims about audience size
- sponsor package commitments
- follow-up after sensitive objection

## Outputs

- Sponsor brief
- Outreach sequence
- CRM update
- Reply classification
- Call prep memo

## Success metrics

- sponsor conversations booked
- LOIs signed
- cash collected
- sponsor renewal probability
